### New Rules

| Rule ID  | Category    | Severity | Notes                                                                      |
|----------|-------------|----------|----------------------------------------------------------------------------|
| TBTRA001 | Usage       | Error    | When using Tombatron.Results.Result<T> you must handle Ok<T> and Error<T>. |
